What is the Motor Vehicle Tax (MVT)?

According to the Motor Vehicle Tax Law, it is the vehicle tax that vehicle owners are obliged to pay. Taxes are paid for all motor vehicles, including land, sea and air vehicles. Tax the obligation is to start with the registration of the vehicles and continue until the registration is completed is doing.Motor Vehicle Tax for road vehicles registered according to the Highway Traffic Law (MTV) is being paid.
